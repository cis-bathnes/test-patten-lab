Beechen Cliff School

Beechen Cliff School is a unique school in many respects. We aim to provide the best possible education for each pupil, to prepare each pupil for adulthood and to set the highest possible standards in all areas of school life; academic, sporting and beyond.

We know every pupil. Individuals matter and visitors comment on the friendliness and confidence of the pupils.
